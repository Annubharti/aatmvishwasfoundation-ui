About Us

We, a group of education and social enthusiasts, came together to start a learning center in Amagachi village of Arariya district in Bihar, which area is highly deprived of education, health, and development. Vijay Paswan, who belongs to Amagachi, started the learning center in October 2022 with limited resourse and strongest desire to serve community.

Our initiative assists around 50-60 kids, many of whom lack access to formal education.
We prioritize strengthening their foundational studies to provide them with at least basic education.
Our secondary goal involves connecting youth with different NGOs to offer skill-building opportunities.
Through these partnerships, we aim to help young people enhance their skills and improve their prospects for employment.

All the team members who are working for these initiative are volunteers but also working full-time job to support themselves and their families. It takes a pipeline of organisations to let problem solvers live up to their fullest potential.
