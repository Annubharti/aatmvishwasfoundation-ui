Vijay Kumar Paswan

Founder

I am originally from Katihar, but been residing in Araria since 2010. My journey in the social sector began in 2010 when I joined Jan Jagran Shakti Sangathan, marking the commencement of a transformative path. Over the course of nearly a decade, I dedicated myself to organizing communities and advocating for their rights and empowerment through my work at Jan Jagran Shakti Sangathan.


In July 2021, I transitioned to Project Potential organization, where I contributed for 18 months. Drawing inspiration from my experiences, I co-founded the Aatmvishwas Foundation in July 2022, located in Amgachhi Panchayat of Sikti block, Araria district. Our initiative, the Aatmvishwas Center, focuses on providing free education and fostering youth development in Amgachhi.


Concurrently, I pursued my studies and recently completed my graduation. My educational journey continues, as I aspire to pursue a Master of Social Work, which I plan to commence soon. Through the Aatmvishwas Foundation, I aim to continue my efforts in driving positive change within society.

About Us

A group of education and social enthusiasts initiated a learning center in Amagachi village, Bihar, addressing the region's educational and developmental gaps. Led by Vijay Paswan, the center, established in October 2022 with limited resources, currently supports 50-60 kids without formal school access. The team comprises volunteers juggling full-time jobs to sustain both their families and this meaningful initiative.
